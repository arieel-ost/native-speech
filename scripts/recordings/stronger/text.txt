Yesterday, the weather was really beautiful, so I decided to go for a walk through the park near my house. The trees were green and the birds were singing everywhere. I think spring is my favorite time of the year.
I walked along the river and watched some children playing with a big red ball. They were laughing and having so much fun together. One little girl threw the ball very high, and it landed right in the water. Her father had to reach in and pull it out.
After that, I stopped at a small village shop to buy some bread and a bottle of juice.
